Mission 2: Exact mean or estimate?

Exact mean rule: To find an exact mean, you need every exact data value. A grouped histogram and a box plot do not give all exact values.

Can you find the exact mean?

DisplayExact mean?Why?
Raw listYesAll values are shown.
Dot plotYesEach dot shows a value.
Exact frequency tableYesValues and counts are shown.
Box plotNoOnly min, Q1, median, Q3, max.
Grouped histogramNoOnly interval counts.

Mission 3: Use IQR when data are skewed

Rule: For skewed data or outliers, use median for center and IQR for variability. Range can be pulled by the extreme value.

TV-watching box plot facts

From the box plot: Q1 = 2 and Q3 = 10.

IQR = Q3 − Q1 = 10 − 2 = 8 hours
